Projects

Our education projects teach practical skills that help young people succeed in their academic, business, and civic careers. Our community service projects promote civic engagement and give young people the opportunity to develop leadership skills. Our projects helping families and communities emphasize local investment, self-reliance, and sustainability. And, during times of emergencies, our community of volunteers and private donors bring help to those in need.

Throughout every aspect of our work, our underlying goal is to build trust and mutual understanding among people of all cultures and faiths and thereby bridge divisions within Bosnian society and build relationships that contribute to lasting peace and prosperity in Bosnia and Herzegovina
