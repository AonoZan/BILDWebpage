Our Volunteers

Each year, our projects benefit greatly from our vibrant community of volunteers, some joining us for one volunteer action, some for years. Our volunteers do everything from help plant trees to taking full responsibility for achieving an objective, for example, implementing all aspects of the Doboj Winter Institute.
Through the years, we have developed a core group of volunteers from both BiH and throughout the world, especially the United States and Europe. The criteria for appearing below is that each person has been part of our community for at least two years and is currently active helping us develop and implement our projects.


We are grateful to all those who have helped us in the past, those who appear below and to those who have relatively recently joined us. Our volunteer community is essential to our previous, current and continuing success.
